## Week 3-4: Soft Launch & Seeding

### 7. Free Product Micro-Seeding

> **Owner:** CEO
> **Skills:** `launch-strategy` (ORB framework — this is a Borrowed channel play)
> **Budget:** ~0 PLN (product cost: ~10gr/unit × 30 people × 2-3 samples each = ~10 PLN total)
> **Deliverable:** 20-30 people seeded, tracking spreadsheet live

**Brief:**

This is NOT an influencer campaign. No contracts, no rates, no briefs sent to creators. This is word-of-mouth engineering: put the product in the right hands and let it speak for itself. The product cost is negligible — the only cost is your time.

**Target persona list (aim for 20-30 people across these categories):**

| Category                              | Why they matter                                               | How to find them                                          | Target qty |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- | ---------- |
| Friends of friends in the scene       | Authentic reach, trust-based                                  | Ask your social circle: "Kto by to docenił?"              | 8-10       |
| Local DJs / music producers           | Cultural connectors, large overlap with target audience       | Instagram DMs, local events, Spotify local playlists      | 3-5        |
| Baristas / bartenders                 | Daily touchpoint with young urbanites, can recommend quietly  | Visit cafes in hip neighborhoods (Praga, Kazimierz, etc.) | 3-5        |
| Skaters / streetwear community        | Core Gen Z demo, visual culture, will photograph products     | Skatepark visits, local streetwear IG pages               | 3-5        |
| Small-reach creators (< 5K followers) | Genuine engagement, no gatekeeping, will post if they like it | Search Polish IG for #zioła, #relaks, #naturalny          | 3-5        |

**What to send each person:**

| Item             | Details                                                                                                                                         |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Product          | 2-3 different blends per person (let them compare). Include at least 1 terpene-infused variant.                                                 |
| Handwritten note | "Cześć! To zioo — mieszanki ziołowe z terpnami z konopi. Spróbuj i daj znać co myślisz 🌿" Keep it casual, personal.                            |
| Brand card       | Small card (business card size) with: zioo logo, @zioo.pl Instagram handle, QR code → store, one-liner: "Kompozycje botaniczne. Wysyłka w 24h." |

**What NOT to send:**

- No press release
- No formal "influencer brief" or posting requirements
- No NDAs
- Don't ask them to post. If it's good, they will.

**Delivery method:**

- Hand-deliver if local (strongest — shows you care)
- InPost if remote (fast, cheap, on-brand)

**Tracking spreadsheet (create in Google Sheets):**

| Column    | What to track                          |
| --------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Name      | Who received the sample                |
| Category  | DJ / barista / friend / creator        |
| Date sent | When you shipped/delivered             |
| IG handle | Their Instagram                        |
| Posted?   | Yes/No/Story/Feed/Reel                 |
| Post link | URL to their content                   |
| Reposted? | Did we reshare their content?          |
| Feedback  | What they said (DM, in person)         |
| Bought?   | Did they convert to a paying customer? |

**UGC repost protocol:**

- If someone posts about zioo, DM them: "Dzięki za share! Możemy to udostępnić?" (ask permission)
- Repost to Stories with credit tag
- If it's a Reel or high-quality feed post, ask if you can pin it or use in paid ads later (with credit)
- Save ALL UGC in a dedicated folder — this becomes ad creative in Month 1-2

**Success metrics:**

- 30%+ of seedees try the product within a week
- 15-20% post organically (6-8 organic posts from 30 seeds = good)
- All feedback logged for product improvement

### 8. Community Channels

> **Owner:** Brand Manager
> **Skills:** `launch-strategy` (Owned channel setup from ORB framework)
> **Deliverable:** Live community group with 20+ members

**Brief:**

**Platform choice: Telegram over Discord.**

Rationale: Discord requires account creation and is less common in Poland outside gaming. Telegram is already widely used, has a lower barrier to entry, supports Polish, and is culturally appropriate for the target demo. The audience is cannabis-adjacent — Telegram is where that community already lives in Poland.

If the CEO prefers Discord (e.g., for richer moderation tools or voice channels later), it's a valid choice — but expect slower initial adoption.

**Channel structure:**

| Channel/Topic     | Purpose                                               |
| ----------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| #witamy (Welcome) | Pinned message: what zioo is, group rules, store link |
| #blendy (Blends)  | Discussion about blends, flavor profiles, preferences |
| #feedback         | Product feedback, complaints, suggestions             |
| #pomysły (Ideas)  | Vote on new blend names, flavors, limited editions.   |
| #offtopic         | General chat, keep the community warm                 |

**Pinned welcome message (Polish):**

```
Hej! 👋 Witamy w społeczności zioo.
To miejsce dla ludzi, którzy cenią botaniczne kompozycje i naturalny relaks.

Co tu robimy:
🌿 Dzielimy się opiniami o blendach
💡 Głosujemy na nowe smaki
📦 Pierwsi dowiadujemy się o nowościach

Zasady: szanuj innych, zero spamu, miło tu być.
Sklep: zioo.pl
```

**Moderation rules:**

- No explicit combustion discussion (same regulatory rules apply to community)
- No competitor links
- No spam or self-promotion
- CEO or Brand Manager moderates actively in first 2 months

**Growth triggers (how to get the first 20-30 members):**

1. Invite all seedees from Brief #7 (they're already engaged)
2. Add invite link to the brand card included with samples
3. Add invite link to Instagram bio / link-in-bio
4. Post "Dołącz do naszej społeczności" on Instagram Stories once a week
5. Include invite link in order confirmation emails (once checkout is live)

**Engagement tactics (first 30 days):**

- Post a "Blend tygodnia" (blend of the week) every Monday — discussion prompt
- Run a quick poll: "Jaki blend powinniśmy zrobić następny?" (crowdsource new ideas)
- Share BTS content not posted on socials (exclusive feel)
- Respond to every message within hours — small community must feel personal
- Once 10+ members, ask for volunteers to test new blends early (builds loyalty)

**When to consider Discord:** If the group consistently exceeds 100 active members and needs voice channels, organized threads, and role-based access (e.g., "OG members" vs. new joiners), migrate to Discord. Not sooner.

**Deliverables:**

- [ ] Telegram group created and configured
- [ ] Welcome message pinned
- [ ] Channel topics organized
- [ ] Invite link ready for distribution
- [ ] All seedees invited
- [ ] Brand Manager committed to daily moderation (first 30 days)
