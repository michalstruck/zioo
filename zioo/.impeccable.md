## Design Context

Reference `design-system/` for concrete data and usage terms - for example color values, shadows, based on element size or area.

### Users

Targets the Polish market, looking for botanical blends to substitute traditional tobacco when partaking in marijuana. The goal is to feel relaxed, clear-minded, and stress-free without the negative, anxiety-inducing effects associated with typical products.

### Brand Personality

Surfer-chill, effortless, bold, and stress-free. The tone of voice is concise, punchy, and direct—absolutely avoiding any corporate speak. The brand sells relaxation and "good times", projecting a confident, youth-focused energy.

### Aesthetic Direction

- **Theme:** Mild Pop-Art / Neo-Brutalist in Light Mode. Unapologetically loud but structurally simple.
- **Colors:** Sun-bleached brights with stark contrasts. Washed-out teal (`#2DD4BF`), sunset orange (`#F97316`), pale yellow (`#FEF08A`), grounded with solid black (`#000000`) borders and shadows, set against cream backgrounds (`#FAFAFA`).
- **Typography:** Space Grotesk (massive, condensed headers + clean, highly legible body copy).
- **Anti-references:** The brand explicitly avoids SaaS-style pages, sterility, fakeness, and the overall "lab vibe" of modern corporate design. No emojis as icons; no flat interfaces without depth.

### Design Principles

1. **Unapologetic Neo-Brutalism:** Embrace thick solid black borders (`border-2` or `border-4`) and hard black dropdown shadows (e.g., `4px 4px 0px 0px #000000`) uniformly across components to create a tactile, playful, "sticker-like" presence.
2. **Defiantly Anti-Lab & Anti-Sterile:** Eliminate sterility by leaning heavily into high color-contrast block layouts, abstract pop-art shapes, and vibrant dual-tone effects. The UI must feel like an energetic, culturally-relevant brand rather than a clinical environment.
3. **Implicit Utility via Design:** Due to Polish regulations, rely on loud visual language (terpene profile, herbs in the blend) to implicitly convey the product's actual use case, while strictly using terms like "herbata ziołowa" or "mieszanka do aromatyzacji." _Never reference combustion._
4. **Snappy & Immediate Interactions:** Avoid overly smooth, slow animations. Interactions and hover states should be fast (`150ms-300ms`) with immediate color shifts and slight translate steps (e.g., `hover:-translate-y-1`) to feel mechanical, immediate, and responsive.
5. **Overwhelmingly Bold Typography (Scaled for Readability):** Use Space Grotesk extensively for headers and vital stats. Typography should function not just as readable text, but as a core structural element cutting through the noise.
