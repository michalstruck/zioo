## Week 2-3: Content & Channels Setup

### 4. Social Media Presence

> **Owner:** Brand Manager (setup) + Copywriter (bio copy)
> **Skills:** `social-content`
> **Deliverable:** Fully configured TikTok + Instagram accounts, ready to post

**Brief:**

**Account setup:**

| Platform  | Handle                           | Priority                                                  |
| --------- | -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Instagram | @zioo.pl (preferred) or @zioo_pl | Primary — visual product showcase, carousels, Reels       |
| TikTok    | @zioo.pl                         | Primary — short-form video, discovery engine, Gen Z reach |

If handles are taken, try: @zioo.blends, @ziooherbals, @zioo.herbs. Consistency across platforms is key.

**Bio copy (Polish):**

Write 2-3 options per platform for CEO review. Framework:

```
Line 1: What we are (max 5 words, punchy)
Line 2: Key differentiator or vibe statement
Line 3: CTA or delivery hook
Link: zioo.pl
```

Example:

```
Kompozycje botaniczne 🌿
Terpeny z konopi. Bez uzależnienia.
Wysyłka w 24h 📦
zioo.pl
```

**Tone rules:**

- No emojis overload — max 1-2 per bio, used for visual anchoring not decoration
- No English unless it's a brand term (e.g., "blend" is fine in Polish cannabis culture)
- Run through `humanizer` — must feel like a person wrote it, not a marketing dept
- Apply `COPY_DICTIONARY.md` — zero banned terms

**Profile photo:** Zioo logo on `#FBF9F4` (Antique Paper) background. Ensure it's legible at 110×110px (IG thumbnail size).

**Visual grid strategy (Instagram):**

- Row pattern: Reel → Carousel → Lifestyle photo → repeat
- Color grading: desaturated, warm, earthy tones. Never oversaturated. Match Clinical Naturalism.
- Use product photography from Brief #2 as the backbone
- Text overlays: Public Sans, `#121625` (Soft Ink Black) on semi-transparent `#FBF9F4` overlay

**Daily engagement routine (15-20 min/day — CEO or Brand Manager):**

- Reply to all comments and DMs within 4 hours
- Comment on 5-10 posts from accounts in the scene (cannabis culture, wellness, streetwear, Polish creators)
- Comments must add value — share a perspective, ask a question. Never "Super post!" or fire emojis
- Follow 10-20 relevant accounts per day initially (first 2 weeks, then slow down)

**Deliverables:**

- [ ] Both accounts created and configured
- [ ] Bio copy approved by CEO
- [ ] Profile photo uploaded
- [ ] Link-in-bio set up (direct to zioo.pl, or Linktree if blog exists)
- [ ] Grid style guide documented (1 page: color grading reference, overlay specs, row pattern)

### 5. First Organic Content Batch (5-10 pieces)

> **Owner:** Brand Manager (direction) + Copywriter (captions) + CEO (filming)
> **Skills:** `social-content`, `humanizer`, `marketing-psychology`
> **Deliverable:** 7-10 published posts across TikTok + Instagram

**Brief:**

This is the first content your audience will ever see. It sets the tone. It must feel authentic, visually premium, and effortlessly cool — not corporate, not try-hard, not like a brand that just hired an agency. Think: "a friend who happens to blend really nice herbs and has good taste."

**Content pieces with full direction:**

**Piece 1-2: "Co jest w środku?" unboxing (Reel/TikTok)**

| Spec     | Value                                                                  |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Format   | Vertical video, 9:16, 1080×1920px                                      |
| Duration | 15-25 seconds                                                          |
| Platform | Post to TikTok first, then IG Reel                                     |
| Sound    | Original ASMR audio (crinkling mylar, herbs pouring). No music needed. |

Script outline:

1. (0-3s) HOOK: Close-up of hand holding sealed green mylar. Text overlay: "Co jest w środku?" or sound of package crinkling
2. (3-10s) Tear open the mylar slowly. Camera catches the herbs spilling or being poured onto a marble/wood surface
3. (10-18s) Close-up of the loose herb blend — show texture, colors, individual ingredients recognizable
4. (18-25s) Pull back to reveal the label. End frame: blend name visible, Zioo logo

What NOT to do: don't show the product near rolling papers, a lighter, or anything combustion-related. Don't add commentary about how to "use" it. Let the visual speak.

Caption framework:

```
[Name of blend] — [one-liner about the mood]
[1-2 sentences about the ingredients, what makes it unique]
[CTA: link in bio / "zamów na zioo.pl"]
#zioo #mieszankiziołowe #aromaterapia #terpeny
```

### 6. SEO Content (Blog Posts)

> **Owner:** Copywriter / Product Owner
> **Skills:** `content-strategy`, `copywriting`, `ai-seo`, `schema-markup`, `humanizer`, `copy-editing`
> **Deliverable:** 5 published blog posts on zioo.pl/blog

**Brief:**

These blog posts serve three purposes: (1) capture organic search traffic for relevant Polish queries, (2) get cited by AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini), and (3) educate potential buyers who don't know what terpenes or herbal blends are yet.

**Per-post specs:**

| #   | Title (Polish)                                   | Primary keyword      | Secondary keywords                                     | Internal link to               |
| --- | ------------------------------------------------ | -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------ |
| 1   | "Czym są terpeny i dlaczego warto je znać?"      | terpeny              | profil terpenowy, efekt entourage, terpeny w konopiach | Terpene-infused product pages  |
| 2   | "Mieszanki ziołowe — naturalny sposób na relaks" | mieszanki ziołowe    | zioła na relaks, herbata ziołowa                       | All product pages              |
| 3   | "Aromaterapia ziołowa — jak to działa?"          | aromaterapia ziołowa | mieszanka do aromatyzacji, relaks z ziołami            | Product pages + Post #1        |
| 4   | "Efekt entourage — co to jest i jak działa?"     | efekt entourage      | terpeny, konopie, synergia                             | Post #1 + Terpene products     |
| 5   | "Jak wybrać mieszankę ziołową dla siebie?"       | mieszanka ziołowa    | porównanie mieszanek, relaks, fokus, sen               | All product pages (comparison) |

**Article structure (apply to every post):**

```
H1: [Title — matches primary keyword naturally]

[Opening paragraph: direct answer to the query in 40-60 words.
This is the snippet AI search will extract. Lead with the answer, not background.]

H2: [Subtopic 1]
[2-3 paragraphs, 100-200 words]

H2: [Subtopic 2]
[2-3 paragraphs, use a table or list where appropriate]

H2: [Subtopic 3]
[Include a comparison table or step-by-step if relevant]

H2: FAQ
[3-4 questions in natural language, concise answers. Add FAQPage schema.]

[Final CTA: "Sprawdź nasze mieszanki na zioo.pl" with link to store]
```

**Writing rules:**

- 800-1200 words per post. Not shorter (SEO value drops), not longer (nobody reads 3000-word posts about herbs)
- Polish language throughout
- Each H2 heading should match a question someone would type into Google or ask ChatGPT
- Lead each section with a direct statement (not a question or teaser) — this is what AI extracts
- Include 2-3 specific data points or facts with sources per post (AI citation boost: +37-40%)
- No keyword stuffing — Princeton GEO study shows it actively hurts AI visibility by -10%
- Run every post through `humanizer` then `copy-editing` before publishing
- Apply `COPY_DICTIONARY.md` — zero banned terms

**Technical SEO per post (Senior Software Engineer implements):**

- `FAQPage` schema on the FAQ section
- `Article` schema with author, datePublished, dateModified
- Meta title: [Primary keyword] — Zioo (max 60 chars)
- Meta description: direct benefit statement + CTA (max 155 chars)
- OG image: use a product photo or branded graphic (1200×630px)
- Internal links: every post links to 1-2 product pages and 1-2 other blog posts (builds topic cluster)
- Alt text on all images: descriptive, Polish, includes keyword naturally

**AI Search Optimization (from `ai-seo` skill):**

- Allow all AI crawlers in robots.txt (GPTBot, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot, Google-Extended)
- Don't gate any blog content behind signups or popups
- Structure content so each block is self-contained (works without surrounding context)
- 40-60 word answer blocks at the top of each section for optimal snippet extraction

**Publishing cadence:** 1 post per week during Week 2-5. Then 1-2 per month ongoing.

**Deliverables:**

- [ ] 5 blog posts drafted

- [ ] Each checked against `COPY_DICTIONARY.md`
- [ ] Schema markup implemented
- [ ] Internal linking complete
