## Page Sections — Copywriting Checklist

Work through each section in order. Each section has its own sub-checklist.

---

### 1. Hero Section

**Goal:** Communicate core value in 5 seconds. Get the visitor to scroll or click.

- [ ] **Headline** — one sentence, outcome-focused or problem-focused
  - Formula options: `{Achieve outcome} bez {pain point}` / `{Question highlighting pain point}` / `{Category} dla {audience}`
  - Must be specific, not generic — avoid "naturalne zioła" type blandness
  - Max ~8 words
  - Write 3 headline variants (A/B/C) with rationale for each
- [ ] **Subheadline** — 1–2 sentences expanding the headline, adding specificity
  - Mention: no nicotine, botanical blends, mood-based (or terpene line), fast delivery
  - Max 2 sentences
- [ ] **Primary CTA button**
  - Formula: action verb + what they get → e.g. "Zamów swój blend", "Sprawdź nasze mieszanki", "Odkryj smaki zioo"
  - ❌ Never: "Kliknij tutaj", "Dowiedz się więcej", "Zamów teraz" (too generic)
  - Write 2–3 CTA options

---

### 2. Trust Signals Bar

**Goal:** Reduce purchase anxiety for a brand-new, unknown brand.

No testimonials at launch — use operational trust signals only.

- [ ] Write copy for 3–4 trust signal pills/badges:
  - "Wysyłka w 24h z InPost" ✓
  - "Darmowa dostawa od 34,99 zł" ✓
  - "Płatność BLIK, Apple Pay, Google Pay" ✓
  - Optional 4th: "100% beznikotynowe" or "Zioła apteczne" or "Bez uzależnienia"
- [ ] Keep each signal under 5 words
- [ ] No exclamation marks

---

### 3. Problem / Pain Block

**Goal:** Show you understand the visitor's situation before pitching the solution. Make them feel seen.

- [ ] Open with a rhetorical question or a blunt statement that mirrors customer language (see `product-marketing-context.md` → "Customer Language" section)
  - Examples from context: "Szukam czegoś zamiast tytoniu do jointa", "Tytoń mnie dusi, chcę coś lżejszego"
- [ ] Name the 3 core pain points (pick the sharpest ones):
  - Uzależnienie od tytoniu / nikotyny
  - Harsh smak, kaszel, podrażnione gardło
  - Poczucie winy ("wiem że to niezdrowe, ale nie mam alternatywy")
- [ ] Write a short transition sentence bridging from pain → solution (do NOT oversell here — let the next section do that)
- [ ] ❌ Do NOT use the word "tytoń" as a primary focus — regulatory no-go zone. Frame around the customer's desire, not the competitor

---

### 4. Product Showcase

**Goal:** Give each blend a character. Let the visitor self-select by mood.

4 blend cards — one per blend. Each card needs:

- [ ] **Fresz card:**
  - One-liner mood/benefit (e.g. "Orzeźwienie o każdej porze")
  - Key herbs: Dziewanna, Malina, Mięta, Szałwia, Pokrzywa, Prawoślaz
  - No terpene line for Fresz — only Natural
  - CTA: "Zobacz blend →"

- [ ] **Czill card:**
  - One-liner mood/benefit (e.g. "Odetnij się od hałasu")
  - Key herbs: Dziewanna, Malina, Melisa, Lawenda, Pokrzywa
  - Terpene line: Girl Scout Cookies profile
  - CTA: "Zobacz blend →"

- [ ] **Fokus card:**
  - One-liner mood/benefit (e.g. "Skupienie bez kofein")
  - Key herbs: Dziewanna, Malina, Mięta, Pokrzywa, Prawoślaz
  - Terpene line: Lemon Skunk profile
  - CTA: "Zobacz blend →"

- [ ] **Slep card:**
  - One-liner mood/benefit (e.g. "Cisza, którą czujesz")
  - Key herbs: Dziewanna, Malina, Melisa, Róża, Pokrzywa
  - Terpene line: Purple Punch profile
  - CTA: "Zobacz blend →"

- [ ] Section heading (e.g. "Znajdź swój moment") — short, punchy

---

### 5. Terpene Education Section

**Goal:** Explain what terpenes are and why they matter — simply, visually, without sounding like a lab report. This is a genuine differentiator in Poland.

- [ ] Section headline — punchy, curiosity-triggering (e.g. "Co to są terpeny i dlaczego to zmienia wszystko")
- [ ] Short intro paragraph (max 40–60 words): what terpenes are, where they come from (cannabis strains), what the "efekt entourage" means
  - ❌ No health claims ("terpeny leczą", "pomagają na bezsenność")
  - ✅ Allowed: describe aroma profiles, how terpenes work (energizing, relaxing etc), expected sensory experience, what makes each strain smell/feel distinct
- [ ] 3 terpene profile blurbs (one per infused blend):
  - **Girl Scout Cookies** (Chill) — aroma/feel description, ≤20 words
  - **Lemon Skunk** (Focus) — aroma/feel description, ≤20 words
  - **Purple Punch** (Sleep) — aroma/feel description, ≤20 words
- [ ] Closing micro-CTA or transition to next section

---

### 6. FAQ Block

**Goal:** Handle objections and regulatory concerns before they become purchase blockers. Pre-empt anxiety.

- [ ] Write 5–6 Q&A pairs. Must include:

  | #   | Question to address                                       | Key answer angle                                                                       |
  | --- | --------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
  | 1   | "To jest legalne?"                                        | 100% legalne — zioła apteczne + terpeny z konopi (legalny ekstrakt).                   |
  | 2   | "Czym różni się linia Terpene od Natural?"                | Profil terpenowy z konopi — intensywniejszy aromat, efekt entourage                    |
  | 3   | "Jak szybko dostanę zamówienie?"                          | Wysyłka w 24h, InPost paczkomat lub kurier                                             |
  | 4   | "Ile kosztuje dostawa?"                                   | 13 zł flat, darmowa od 34,99 zł                                                        |
  | 5   | "Dlaczego warto wybrać zioo zamiast czegokolwiek innego?" | Jedyne takie mieszanki w PL — mood-based, z profilem terpenowym, bez nikotyny          |
  | 6   | "Jak używać mieszanki?"                                   | Mieszanka do aromatyzacji — ⚠️ answer MUST be regulatory-safe, no combustion reference |

- [ ] Answers must be short (2–4 sentences max per answer)
- [ ] Q6 answer is the highest-risk item — must be fully compliant with `COPY_DICTIONARY.md`. Run answer past banned terms list manually.
- [ ] Questions should be phrased in first person ("Czy to jest legalne?" not "Is it legal?")

---

### 7. Final CTA Section

**Goal:** Recap the value, reduce last-minute hesitation, push to purchase.

- [ ] Short value recap — 1–2 sentences max (don't re-explain everything, just remind why this is different)
- [ ] Repeat primary CTA button (same as hero or a variant)
- [ ] Optional: Mention "Zestaw Startowy" as the easiest entry point for undecided visitors ("Nie wiesz od czego zacząć? Zamów Zestaw Startowy — wszystkie blendy w jednym.")
- [ ] Optional trust signal reminder: "Zamówienie dzisiaj = dostawa jutro"
- [ ] ❌ No exclamation marks, no "Nie czekaj!", no urgency manufactured from nothing

## Post-Draft Checklist — Humanizer

Run the `humanizer` skill on the complete draft before submitting.

- [ ] Scan for and remove AI vocabulary: "additionally", "crucial", "delve", "enhance", "fostering", "showcase", "tapestry", "testament", "underscore", "vibrant", "pivotal", "landscape" — and their Polish equivalents
- [ ] Scan for rule-of-three overuse — break up triplets that feel formulaic
- [ ] Scan for em dash overuse — replace with commas or rephrase
- [ ] Scan for promotional, advertisement-like language ("boasts", "nestled", "breathtaking") — remove
- [ ] Check for excessive boldface in body copy — reduce
- [ ] Check sentence rhythm — vary length, don't let every sentence be the same structure
- [ ] Read the full copy aloud (or mentally) — does it sound like a real Polish person talking, or an AI?
- [ ] Verify the copy has a point of view and a personality, not just neutral information
