# zioo Copy Dictionary

This dictionary defines strict rules for terminology used across the zioo product, marketing, and UI. We operate under strict Polish market regulations, which means our products must be positioned clearly as botanical blends, not for combustion, or consumption as tea/infusion.

---

## 🚨 PRODUCT CATEGORY — NON-NEGOTIABLE

**Our products are STRICTLY: `mieszanka do aromatyzacji`**

This is a regulatory requirement and cannot be changed, negotiated, or softened.

- **DO NOT** position our products as tea, herbal tea, or suggest brewing/drinking as a primary use case.
- **DO NOT** use `herbata ziołowa` or `herbata` as a product descriptor — these terms position us as a food product, which we are not cleared for.
- **DO NOT** use `napar` (infusion/brew) as a usage instruction — it implies the product is consumed as a drink.

---

## 🚫 BANNED TERMS

Do **NOT** use any of the following terms or their derivatives under any circumstances:

- palenie (smoking)
- palić (to smoke)
- waporyzacja (vaporization)
- waporyzować (to vaporize)
- inhalacja (inhalation)
- dym (smoke)
- bletki / bibułki (rolling papers)
- tytoń (tobacco) — _never market or refer to our product as tobacco substitute or meant for combustion_
- nikotyna
- joint, skręt i tym podobne
- herbata (tea) — _do not position product as tea; regulatory risk_
- herbata ziołowa (herbal tea) — _banned as product descriptor_
- napar (infusion/brew) — _implies food/drink use, not compliant_

---

## ✅ ALLOWED & PREFERRED TERMS

Use the following terms to describe our products and their usage:

- **mieszanka do aromatyzacji** (aromatization blend) — _the ONLY allowed product category term_
- mieszanka ziołowa (herbal blend) — _allowed as informal description, NOT as official product category_
- relaks (relaxation)
- odprężenie (unwinding)
- aromaterapia (aromatherapy)
- profil terpenowy (terpene profile) — _for the infused line_
- kompozycja botaniczna (botanical composition)
- mentions of chemical compounds found in herbs
- effects of chemical compounds found in herbs
- **saszetka 0.5g (0.5g sachet)** — _always emphasize single-use, perfect portion_
- **zawsze świeże (always fresh)**
- **idealna porcja na raz (perfect single-use portion)**

---

## Agent Usage Instructions

When generating or refining copy, always apply this dictionary alongside the `@clarify` and `@humanizer` skills.

1. **@clarify**: Ensure the distinction between our product and combustion products is explicit and unambiguous. Keep the tone punchy, direct, and surfer-chill.
2. **@humanizer**: Remove any overly corporate or artificially "lab-like" phrasing. The tone must remain bold, confident, and youth-focused.
3. **Terminology check**: Before finalizing any copy, scan for banned terms. If `herbata`, `herbata ziołowa`, or `napar` appears as a product descriptor or usage instruction, replace with `mieszanka do aromatyzacji` or `urządzenie aromatyzujące`.
