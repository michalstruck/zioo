# Product Vision: Zioo

## 1. High-Level Vision & Core Identity

**Brand Vibe:** Surfer-chill, effortless, bold, and stress-free. We sell relaxation, good times, and clear minds without the anxiety often associated with marijuana.
**Visual Direction:** Mild Pop-Art / Neo-Brutalist. Unapologetically loud but structurally simple. It is designed to cut through the noise and immediately establish a laid-back personality.
**Tone of Voice:** Concise, punchy, direct. No corporate speak. We use simple language that resonates with our target demographic.

### Design System (The "Chill" Era)

- **Colors:** Sun-bleached brights with stark contrasts. Washed-out teal, sunset orange, pale yellow, and solid black for grounding.
- **Typography:** Massive, condensed sans-serif for loud headers. Clean, highly legible sans-serif for body copy.
- **UI Components:** "Sticker-like" buttons, cards with thick black borders, and hard black drop shadows. Elements should feel tactile, slightly retro, and playful.
- **Imagery:** Simple, abstract shapes or pop-art illustrations representing the herbal blends and their distinct vibes.

---

## 2. Regulatory Compliance & Constraints (CRITICAL)

Zioo operates in the Polish market under strict legal constraints regarding how products can be marketed.

- **Zero Tolerance:** Do NOT use words like "palenie" (smoking), "waporyzacja" (vaporizing), or any direct synonyms indicating combustion or inhalation.
- **Mandatory Terminology:** All copy must strictly position the products as "herbata ziołowa" (herbal tea) or "mieszanka do aromatyzacji" (aromatization blend).
- **Implicit Messaging:** The UI/UX, aesthetic, and specific descriptors (like terpene profiles) do the heavy lifting to imply the product's actual use case (recreational pairing) without explicitly stating it.

## 3. Product Catalog

Our micro-storefront focuses on an exclusive, highly curated selection of two major lines, featuring 3 botanical blends each (6 products total).

**Line 1: Infused (Terpene-Enriched)** and **Line 2: Natural (No Terpenes)**

The 3 base profiles are:

1.  **"Deep Unwind" (Sleep & Relaxation)**
    - _Profile:_ 20% Damiana, 45% Dziewanna Liść, 20% Malina Liść, 15% Melisa
    - _Terpene (Infused Line):_ Purple Punch
2.  **"Social Spark" (Euphoria & Mood Enhancement)**
    - _Profile:_ 10% Róża Kwiat, 45% Dziewanna, 10% Lawenda Kwiat, 25% Prawoślaz Liść, 10% Rumianek Kwiat
    - _Terpene (Infused Line):_ GSC (Girl Scout Cookies)
3.  **"Clear Mind" (Focus & Clarity)**
    - _Profile:_ 15% Mięta, 40% Dziewanna, 20% Pokrzywa, 25% Malina Liść
    - _Terpene (Infused Line):_ Lemon Skunk

## 4. Epic & Feature Roadmap

### Epic 1: Brand & Visual Foundation (Pop-Art System)

- Define the exact color palette (hex codes for sun-bleached brights and stark blacks).
- Select and implement the typography stack (massive headers + clean body).
- Build the core UI component library (buttons, cards, inputs) with the signature thick borders and hard black shadows.

### Epic 2: Global Architecture & Navigation

- **Header / Navigation:** Persistent, minimalistic header containing links to "About" (Landing Page), "Store", and "Blog" (dummy placeholder for now).
- **Cart Visibility:** A highly visible cart indicator in the header that makes the cart state immediately apparent at all times.

### Epic 3: The Landing Page (The Vibe Check)

- **Hero Section:** Overwhelmingly large, bold headline with pop-art illustrations of the blends.
- **The Pitch:** 3-4 massive bullet points (Why Zioo? Better vibe, no anxiety, pure taste).
- **Separation:** Keep the landing page strictly for storytelling and vibe checking. Storefront will be separate.
- **Compliance Integration:** Naturally but firmly embed the "herbata ziołowa" / "mieszanka do aromatyzacji" terminology.

### Epic 4: The Micro-Storefront (Fast & Loud)

- **Dedicated Store Page:** Separate from the landing page, minimalistic but on-brand.
- **Product Display:** Big, bold cards for the 6 distinct products (3 blends × 2 lines: Natural & Infused).
- **Product Details:** Punchy stats, emphasizing composition percentages and terpene profiles (for the infused line) using large numbers.
- **Cart Experience:** Overhauled cart visibility. Slide-out drawer with a huge "Checkout" button for zero-friction conversion, feeling robust and extremely obvious to the user.

### Epic 5: Blog & Content (Future Proofing)

- **Blog Scaffolding:** Simple, readable layout for future articles aimed at SEO and education.
- **Initial State:** Dummy content / "Coming Soon" styling.

### Epic 6: Checkout & Operations (No Stress)

- **Payments:** Prominent integration of BLIK and InPost Paczkomaty (Polish market essentials).
- **Disclaimers:** Legal text (aromatization only) styled to fit the pop-art aesthetic, highly visible in the footer.
