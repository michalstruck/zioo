## Design Context

Reference `design-system/` for concrete data and usage terms - for example color values, shadows, based on element size or area.

### Users

Targets the Polish market, looking for botanical blends to substitute traditional tobacco when partaking in marijuana. The goal is to feel relaxed, clear-minded, and stress-free without the negative, anxiety-inducing effects associated with typical products.

### Brand Personality

Surfer-chill, effortless, bold, and stress-free. The tone of voice is concise, punchy, and direct—absolutely avoiding any corporate speak. The brand sells relaxation and "good times", projecting a confident, youth-focused energy.

- **Anti-references:** The brand explicitly avoids SaaS-style pages, sterility, fakeness, and the overall "lab vibe" of modern corporate design. No emojis as icons; no flat interfaces without depth.

### Design Principles

3. **Implicit Utility via Design:** Due to Polish regulations, rely on loud visual language (terpene profile, herbs in the blend) to implicitly convey the product's actual use case, while strictly using terms like "herbata ziołowa" or "mieszanka do aromatyzacji." _Never reference combustion._
